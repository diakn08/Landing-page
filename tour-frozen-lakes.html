This 5-day journey takes you through the heart of Kyrgyzstan, from Bishkek to Naryn and the stunning Kel-Suu Lake at 3,200 m. Explore Kegeti Gorge and Chon Kemin National Park, enjoy horseback riding, watch the traditional Kok Boru game, visit frozen lakes for photography and ice activities, and see the historic Tash Rabat caravanserai. Experience breathtaking mountain landscapes and warm Kyrgyz hospitality in cozy guesthouses and hotels.

Highlights:

  • Kegeti Gorge: Scenic walk and photography opportunities.
  • Chon Kemin: Two-hour horseback ride and traditional Kok Boru horse game.
  • Orto-Tok & Ara Kol: Frozen lakes, photography, and ice activities.
  • Tash Rabat: Historic stone caravanserai in the mountains.
  • Kel-Suu: Walk on the frozen lake and stunning mountain landscapes.
  • Kok-Moinok Canyon: Scenic canyon on the way back to Bishkek.

Program of the tour: Kyrgyzstan Frozen Lakes
Kel-Suu & Ara-Kol & Orto-Tokoy

Day 1: Bishkek - Chon-Kemin January 10th

+

Start your journey from Bishkek and travel to Chon-Kemin National Park. Enjoy scenic views along the way and settle into your guesthouse. In the afternoon, experience traditional Kyrgyz culture with a horseback riding session and watch the exciting Kok Boru horse game.

Accommodation: Guesthouse in Chon-Kemin

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Day 2: Chon Kemin - Naryn January 11th

+

After breakfast, continue your journey to Naryn. Stop at Kegeti Gorge for stunning photography opportunities. Arrive in Naryn and check into your hotel. Explore the local area and enjoy traditional Kyrgyz cuisine for dinner.

Accommodation: Hotel in Naryn

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Day 3: Naryn - Kel-Suu January 12th

+

Today's highlight is the magnificent Kel-Suu Lake at 3,200 meters. Walk on the frozen lake surface and capture incredible photographs of the surrounding mountain landscapes. Visit the historic Tash Rabat caravanserai. Return to Naryn for the night.

Accommodation: Hotel in Naryn

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Day 4: Kel-Suu - Naryn January 13th

+

Spend more time at Kel-Suu for photography and ice activities. Experience the tranquility of the frozen lake and dramatic mountain scenery. Return to Naryn in the evening and enjoy a traditional dinner with locals.

Accommodation: Hotel in Naryn

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Day 5: Naryn - Bishkek January 14th

+

After breakfast, begin your return journey to Bishkek. Stop at Kok-Moinok Canyon for scenic views and photo opportunities. Arrive in Bishkek in the evening, concluding your unforgettable winter adventure in Kyrgyzstan.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Please note: some areas we visit during the tour may experience temperatures as low as 40 degrees below zero. We recommend being well-prepared and dressed for such conditions.
